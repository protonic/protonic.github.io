## About Temp-Mail.fyi

I am the creator of Temp-Mail.fyi, a privacy-focused temporary email service designed to help internet users protect their primary email identity from spam, phishing campaigns, and unwanted marketing messages.

Temp-Mail.fyi was developed after researching how user email identities are collected, stored, and shared across the modern internet ecosystem. The platform was designed to address privacy concerns affecting users across multiple categories of internet services, including social platforms, online marketplaces, newsletters, software trials, discount websites, developer tools, online forums, and promotional campaigns.

By providing a temporary mailbox that automatically expires, users can interact with websites while minimizing long-term exposure of their personal inbox.

Temp-Mail.fyi is intended for privacy protection and testing purposes. Users should not use temporary email services to violate the terms of service of other platforms, bypass security systems, or conduct malicious activity. The platform promotes responsible internet use and aims to help individuals maintain greater control over their digital privacy.

Temp-Mail.fyi is an independent project maintained by me and is continuously improved to enhance privacy, reliability, and usability. Feedback, suggestions, and responsible security disclosures are always welcome.
