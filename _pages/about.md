I am an IT Infrastructure and Security Consultant with more than 15 years of experience designing, securing, and modernizing enterprise environments across cloud, virtualization, cybersecurity, automation, and large-scale operations.

My work sits at the intersection of strategic architecture and hands-on execution. I have led datacenter migrations, enterprise virtualization programs, cloud modernization initiatives, and security uplift efforts for complex corporate and retail environments where uptime, compliance, and operational discipline are non-negotiable.

## What I Do

- Architect cloud and virtualization platforms using Microsoft Azure, VMware, Citrix DaaS, Citrix Virtual Apps, and Microsoft Entra ID
- Strengthen enterprise security with CyberArk, Sophos, Rapid7, CrowdStrike Falcon, and modern identity controls
- Administer and modernize Windows and Linux infrastructure, including Active Directory, DNS, DHCP, and server lifecycle transitions
- Build automation frameworks in PowerShell and MECM to reduce repetitive operational work and improve consistency
- Support ITIL-aligned operations with change management, root cause analysis, monitoring, and stakeholder communication

## Enterprise Impact

Across my consulting career, I have worked on large-scale transformations that combine architecture, delivery, and operational governance.

- Orchestrated datacenter and infrastructure migrations involving physical servers and hundreds of production and test virtual machines
- Implemented privileged access controls and service account protections to reduce exposure and strengthen Zero Trust posture
- Enabled Microsoft 365 email security controls such as DKIM and DMARC, along with Conditional Access and multi-factor authentication
- Drove automation initiatives that reduced manual administrative workload by up to 75 percent
- Led remediation and hardening efforts across thousands of vulnerabilities in enterprise environments
